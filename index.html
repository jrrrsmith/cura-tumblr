Yolanda had been sober for years, on a healing path with yoga, meditation and plant medicine, when she was devastated by the death of her youngest son in an Oakland jail. Cura is an interdimensional documentary about a Mexican American mother called by her spirit guides to seek healing in dreams, visions and community as she fights to liberate her oldest son Donny from a life of addiction and end the cycle of violence and chaos in her family and community.
